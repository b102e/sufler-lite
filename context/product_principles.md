# Product Principles — Суфлер Lite

1. Anxiety reduction > feature richness
2. The human is always the speaker — AI only suggests
3. Choice UI > typing
4. One question / one action at a time
5. Mobile-first, one-thumb interaction
6. Dark theme, calm, minimal
7. No emojis. No icons. Text only.
8. Russian as the primary language of the interface
9. Italian as the language of generated call responses
10. Buttons are the primary interaction surface
11. Transcript is secondary — suggestions are primary
12. Speed > completeness during live calls
13. Graceful degradation — always show something useful, never crash silently
14. Invisible intelligence — the AI should feel like a calm human helper, not a robot
